J Balvin, whose real name is José Álvaro Osorio Balvin, is from Medellin, Colombia. He is a regaetton singer. He moved to the US with friend when he was 17 to learn English and pursue music. Over the next 10 years, he traveled back and forth between the two coutries, learning English and working in different clubs and venues. He caught his big break in 2014 with "6 AM," a song that peaked at No. 2, exploding his career. In June 2017,"Mi Gente" was released and became the No. 1 song on Spotify. It was later remixed with Beyonce.
